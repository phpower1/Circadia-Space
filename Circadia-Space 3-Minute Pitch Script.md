# **Circadia-Space: The 3-Minute Pitch Script**

**Objective:** To convince the OSMED judges that Circadia-Space is a high-impact, technologically sophisticated, and financially viable solution for both Space Medicine and Terrestrial Public Health.

### **\[0:00-0:30\] The Hook & The Problem**

*(Start with a confident, measured tone)*

"Good morning. My name is \[Your Name\], and I’m here from **Circadia-Space**.

In the next three minutes, I want to talk to you about a silent toxin. It affects 99% of the U.S. population and every single astronaut we send into orbit. It’s not a chemical or a virus—it’s **Artificial Light at Night**.

On Earth, light pollution suppresses melatonin and shreds our circadian rhythms, leading to a 30% increase in cardiovascular disease and costing the U.S. healthcare system nearly **$95 billion dollars** every year. In space, this same disruption leads to cognitive decline and metabolic drift that threatens mission success.

We are here to solve the 'Silent Circadian Crisis' from Boston to the stars."

### **\[0:30-1:15\] The Solution: Lumina-Check AI**

*(Shift to an innovative, tech-forward tone)*

"Introducing **Lumina-Check AI**.

We’ve built an agentic, multimodal intelligence platform that performs what we call 'Circadian Efficiency Audits.'

Using our Next.js interface, a user—whether a resident in Quincy or an astronaut in a lunar habitat—uploads a simple photo of their environment.

Our **Vision Agent** immediately analyzes the spectral glare and Bortle scale, while our backend agent—hosted on **Modal.com**—queries **NASA’s Black Marble satellite data** in real-time. We cross-reference ground-level light trespass with orbital radiance to generate a 'Personal Circadian Health Audit.'

This isn't just a map. It’s an actionable diagnostic tool that recommends precise lighting interventions to restore human biological health."

### **\[1:15-2:15\] The Business Model & ROI**

*(Shift to a professional, consultancy/business tone)*

"But Circadia-Space is more than just a tool; it’s a scalable consultancy.

Our business model targets three high-value tiers:

1. **First, Municipal Health:** We offer Massachusetts cities like Boston and Cambridge localized risk mapping for $25k to $100k per audit. This identifies 'High-Trespass' zones where streetlight retrofitting yields the highest public health ROI.  
2. **Second, Healthcare Payers:** We partner with insurers like Blue Cross Blue Shield to integrate our AI into wellness programs. By identifying at-risk members in 'High-Light' zip codes, we can prevent chronic disease, saving insurers an estimated **$7,000 per member, per year**.  
3. **Third, The Space Sector:** We license our 'Circadian Diagnostic Agent' to aerospace leaders like SpaceX and Blue Origin to automate habitat lighting systems based on real-time crew health markers.

Our operational efficiency is driven by AI—replacing months of manual environmental studies with automated, low-latency intelligence."

### **\[2:15-3:00\] The Vision & Close**

*(End with an inspiring, authoritative tone)*

"Our roadmap is clear. By Q3 of 2026, we will launch the 'Boston Light-Census' pilot. By 2027, we will be the standard for circadian care in Massachusetts and the preferred intelligence layer for LEO commercial habitats.

Space is the ultimate laboratory for human health. By solving the extreme lighting challenges of a Moon base, we are creating a hyper-efficient health model for the millions of people living under the glow of our city skylines.

At **Circadia-Space**, we don't just map the stars; we protect the biological rhythms that allow us to reach them.

Thank you."

### **Pitch Delivery Tips:**

* **Pacing:** Aim for about 130-150 words per minute.  
* **Visuals:** When mentioning "NASA Black Marble," have a slide showing the glowing Earth at night to emphasize the scale.  
* **The "Agent" Mention:** Briefly mention that the AI does the work of 10 human researchers—this justifies the "Efficiency Audit" price tag.
