# **Circadia-Space: AI-Driven Scotobiology for Earth & Orbit**

> **Scotobiology** is the scientific study of the biological effects of darkness and light cycles on living organisms — particularly how artificial light disrupts natural circadian rhythms.

**A Business Plan for the 2026 Massachusetts Space Week Space Medicine Hackathon**

## **1\. Executive Summary**

**Circadia-Space** is a Massachusetts-based AI health consultancy that specializes in "Circadian Efficiency Audits." We utilize an agentic, multimodal AI platform, **Lumina-Check**, to bridge the gap between NASA’s Earth-observation data and individual human health. By mapping the impact of Light Pollution (Artificial Light at Night \- ALAN) on human circadian rhythms, we provide actionable intelligence to healthcare payers, municipal governments, and aerospace contractors. Our mission is to protect the human biological clock—the foundation of all metabolic and mental health—from the neighborhoods of Boston to the future habitats of Mars.

## **2\. The Problem: The "Silent" Circadian Crisis**

### **Terrestrial Impact**

Light pollution affects 99% of the U.S. population. Exposure to artificial light at night suppresses melatonin, leading to chronic sleep deprivation and metabolic mismatch.

* **Health Risk:** Linked to a 30-50% increase in cardiovascular events, obesity, type 2 diabetes, and hormone-dependent cancers.  
* **Economic Cost:** Sleep disorders cost the U.S. healthcare system **$94.9 billion annually** in direct spending, with an additional **$411 billion** lost in national productivity.

### **Space Medicine Impact**

Astronauts on long-duration missions lack the Earth’s natural 24-hour light cycle. Without sophisticated circadian regulation, crews face:

* **Cognitive Decline:** Impaired decision-making due to sleep fragmentation.  
* **Physiological Drift:** Long-term metabolic instability during deep-space travel.

## **3\. The Solution: Lumina-Check AI**

Lumina-Check is an autonomous, agentic workflow that performs hyper-localized scotobiological audits.

1. **Data Ingestion:** Users upload night-sky imagery and sleep metrics via a Next.js interface.  
2. **Multimodal Vision Analysis:** A Gemini-powered Vision Agent analyzes spectral glare and estimates the local Bortle Scale.  
3. **Geospatial Fusion:** A backend agent (hosted on [Modal.com](https://modal.com)) queries **NASA’s Black Marble (VIIRS)** API to cross-reference ground-level data with satellite-level radiance.  
4. **Actionable Health Reports:** The AI generates a "Personal Circadian Health Audit" that recommends specific lighting interventions (e.g., spectral shielding, blue-light curfews) to restore melatonin production.

## **4\. Technical Architecture & Efficiency**

Our competitive advantage lies in our **Agentic Workflow**, which replaces expensive manual environmental studies with automated, low-latency AI analysis.

* **Frontend:** Next.js (Tailwind CSS) for a high-fidelity "Command Center" dashboard.  
* **Backend:** [Modal.com](https://modal.com) for high-performance, asynchronous geospatial processing.  
* **Logic:** Coordinator-Worker AI model using Google’s multimodal models for vision and health-data correlation.  
* **Scalability:** An "Offline-First" architecture ensures the system can run locally on a spacecraft or in a modular habitat, syncing data only when connectivity is available.
